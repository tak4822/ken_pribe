Gnomes of the Cheese Forest and Other Poems

Open this book and embark on a journey where gnomes sing cheese into existence, where witches, trolls and monsters lurk in every corner, and where fantasy and reality become a blur of whimsy and wonder. Discover the mysteries of the Cricket Watcher, the Smoggle-Wogg, and the Thing in Skegemog Lake, party at the Rock n’ Roll Pizza Palace, hear the epic tale of brave Professor Stitch-Wicket, and meet Sebastian J. Thrupp, the boy who looked up.

All of these characters and many more are found in this imaginative collection of poems and drawings, coming soon from author & illustrator Ken Priebe.
